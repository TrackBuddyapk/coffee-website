Our Story

A modern coffee bar built on craft

At Mercado Coffee Bar, every cup is brewed with intention. We pour clean, bold espresso, hand-blend creamy frappes, whisk vibrant ceremonial matcha, and slow-extract single-origin beans through the V60 method. Whether you're after a comforting Spanish latte or an adventurous Blue Matcha Elixir, our baristas are here to guide you to your perfect cup.

  • Signature roasts balanced for depth & comfort
  • Hand-crafted frappes, lattes & matcha
  • V60 hand-brew experience for true coffee lovers
